THE BENEFITS OF EXERCISE

Exercise can enhance the performance of the heart, lungs, and blood vessels, as well as aim to prevent heart disease, high blood pressure, and stroke. It is recommended to exercise 3-5 times a week, with moderate intensity or 65%-75% of your maximum heart rate. Perform exercise for 20-30 minutes, and if your capacity allows, it can be extended to one hour.
